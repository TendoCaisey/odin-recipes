Coconut Rum Punch

Description

Shake up this coconut rum punch on a sunny afternoon and you will feel like you're in the tropics. Fresh lime and fresh pineapple garnish this sparkling drink.

Ingredients

  • ice, as needed
  • 2 fluid ounces rum
  • 2 fluid ounces pineapple coconut juice
  • 1/2 lime, juiced
  • 1 tablespoon agave syrup
  • ginger ale or lemon lime soda, such as SpriteÂ®, as needed
  • 1 lime slice
  • 1/3 pineapple ring

Steps

  1. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice; add rum, coconut pineapple juice, lime juice, and agave syrup. Shake until the outside of shaker is frosted, about 30 seconds.
  2. Pour into a glass filled with ice, and top off with ginger ale. Garnish with lime and pineapple.
